The Household Rhythm

3 Bedrooms • 2 Baths • 3 Cats • Basement Business • Yard

Daily Maintenance

Small habits to eliminate deep-clean weekends.

✨ Nightly 10-Min Reset

Load dishwasher / Wash dishes
Wipe counters & stove
Put food away
Quick sweep kitchen floor
Pick up clutter & fold blankets
Straighten pillows
Scoop litter boxes & shake mats
Wipe bathroom sink & straighten towels
Move laundry to dryer or fold small load
Entry / Kitchen sweep

🐾 Pet & Kitchen (Daily)

Wash dishes / Run dishwasher
Wipe counters & stovetop
Sweep crumbs
Scoop litter boxes
Sweep litter area
Small load of laundry

Weekly Standard

Resetting the house for the week ahead.

🧽 20-Min Bathroom Method

Spray shower, tub, sink, toilet (Let sit)
Clean mirrors & light fixtures
Wipe sink & counters
Scrub shower & tub
Clean toilet (bowl, seat, base)
Mop floor on the way out

🏠 House & Kitchen

Mop kitchen floor
Clean microwave interior
Wipe appliance fronts
Change bedding
Vacuum bedrooms & main floors
Vacuum rugs & furniture
Dust furniture surfaces
Vacuum & clean around litter boxes

Biweekly

Every two weeks tasks.

🔍 Detail Cleaning

Vacuum under furniture edges
Clean baseboards (high traffic)
Wash pet bedding
Clean window sills
Wipe interior doors & handles

Monthly Deep Dive

Focus on systems and hidden areas.

🧼 Deep Clean Items

Clean fridge shelves & door seals
Clean dishwasher filter
Clean oven interior
Clean bathroom drains & grout scrub
Dust air vents & air returns
Wash trash cans
Vacuum behind toilets
Deep clean litter area & wash mats

🛠️ Critical Maintenance

Replace/Clean HVAC filter
Test smoke & CO detectors
Check for plumbing leaks (sinks/toilets)
Inspect basement for moisture/pests
Inspect washing machine hoses
Check sump pump pit

Quarterly & Seasonal

Once every 3-4 months.

🛋️ Rare Deep Cleans

Wash walls (high traffic) & mop walls
Clean baseboards throughout house
Clean window tracks & blinds
Wash curtains
Move furniture and vacuum underneath
Clean behind fridge & stove

📦 Garage & Systems

Sweep garage floor & organize tools
Clean fridge coils
Inspect caulking (tubs/showers)
Run ice/citrus through disposal
Check water heater for leaks/rust
Clean AC condensate drain line
Test GFCI outlets

Twice Per Year & Annual

Big picture home preservation.

📅 Every 6 Months

Flush water heater
Clean dryer vent and duct
Clean gutters & yard debris cleanup
Pressure wash exterior surfaces
Check weather stripping (doors/windows)
Lubricate garage door tracks

🏆 Once Per Year

Professional HVAC service
Inspect roof, chimney, & siding
Wash interior & exterior windows
Deep clean basement & garage walls
Clean inside cabinets & closets
Inspect attic insulation & ventilation

Yard Maintenance

🍂 Seasonal

Mulch beds

Check yard drainage
